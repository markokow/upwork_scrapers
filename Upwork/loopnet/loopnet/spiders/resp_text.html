About Los Angeles

Los Angeles, the West Coasts mega city, is the second largest metropolitan area in the nation, with over 10 million people as well as a dynamic and diverse economy larger than many nations. As the entertainment capital of the world, the city is a magnet for talent and creativity. Los Angeles is also one of the major centers in the U.S. for technology and startups. Possessing the largest ports on the West Coast, the city is a global center for international trade. Most goods coming from Asia enter the county through the ports of Los Angeles and Long Beach.

The metro possesses a relatively large number of affluent households. The city is also a magnet for tourists, drawing more than 50 million tourists in a typical year. Los Angeles benefits from several esteemed educational institutions in the area. The University of California, Los Angeles, University of Southern California, and the California Institute of Technology are among the best universities in the world.

Los Angeles boasts an unparalleled lifestyle. The weather is among the best in the nation, with residents enjoying over 280 days of sunshine a year and a temperate climate. Additionally, the options for residents to seek out nature are unrivalled. Stunning beaches, enchanting desert landscapes, and majestic mountains are all visitable in a single day. Great hiking, surfing, and skiing are all within easy reach.
