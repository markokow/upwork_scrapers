Question

The type of paper used in packaging items for delivery is ___.
  1. pH paper
  2. filter paper
  3. cardboard
  4. none of these


Solution

The correct option is C cardboard
Cardboard boxes are primarily used for packaging goods and materials. They can also be recycled. Among the given options, the type of paper used in packaging items for delivery is cardboard.
